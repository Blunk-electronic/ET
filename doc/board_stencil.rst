Stencil
-------

Drawing Objects
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

	.. code-block::
		
		draw stencil top line 1 70 55  60 66
	
	.. code-block::
	
		draw stencil top arc 2 100 100  50 100  150 100 ccw

	.. code-block::
		
		draw stencil top circle 2 100 100  20
		
	.. code-block::
	
		draw stencil top zone line 0 70 line 10 70 line 10 80 line 0 80

		

Moving Objects
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

	|VNS| m i

	
		
Deleting Objects
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

	|VNS| del i
